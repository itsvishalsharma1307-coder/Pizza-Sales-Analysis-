# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard | Power BI & SQL

## 📌 Overview
A pizza restaurant chain wants to make data-driven decisions to improve sales 
and understand customer behavior. This project builds an interactive Power BI 
dashboard backed by SQL queries to track KPIs and visualize sales trends 
across categories, sizes, and time periods.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **SQL** – Data querying and analysis
- **Power BI** – Dashboard and visualizations
- **Excel/CSV** – Raw data source

## 📊 Problem Statement

### KPI Requirements
- **Total Revenue** – Sum of total price of all orders
- **Average Order Value** – Total revenue ÷ Total orders
- **Total Pizzas Sold** – Sum of all pizza quantities sold
- **Total Orders** – Total number of orders placed
- **Average Pizzas Per Order** – Total pizzas ÷ Total orders

### Charts Requirements
- Daily trend of total orders (Bar Chart)
- Monthly trend of total orders (Line Chart)
- % of sales by pizza category (Pie Chart)
- % of sales by pizza size (Pie Chart)
- Total pizzas sold by category (Funnel Chart)
- Top 5 best sellers – revenue, quantity & orders
- Bottom 5 worst sellers – revenue, quantity & orders

### Sales Trends
- 📅 Orders are **highest on weekends** (Friday & Saturday evenings)
- 📆 **July and January** have the maximum monthly orders

### Category & Size Performance
- 🏆 **Classic category** contributes to maximum sales & total orders
- 📏 **Large size** pizzas contribute to maximum sales

### Best Sellers 🥇
- **Revenue** – The Thai Chicken Pizza
- **Quantity** – The Classic Deluxe Pizza
- **Total Orders** – The Classic Deluxe Pizza

### Worst Sellers 📉
- **Revenue** – The Brie Carre Pizza
- **Quantity** – The Brie Carre Pizza
- **Total Orders** – The Brie Carre Pizza
